    Privacy policy at a glance

    The following gives you an overview of what happens to your personal information when you visit this website. Personal information is any data with which you can be personally identified. The operator of this website takes the protection of your personal data serious, treats it confidential and in accordance with the legal data protection regulations.

    Who is responsible for the data collection on this site?

    The site operator and third parties carry out the data processing on this website. The operator's contact data can be found in the website's required legal notice.

    How is your data collected?

    This website stores data in your browser's storages (e. g. as cookies). This is necessary for the website's operation and for remembering your preferences. You can find more information about this here.

    However, the website operator himself will only record your personal data, if you actively send it to him (e. g. via email).

    Third parties can also collect data when you visit this site. You can find more information about this here.

    For what is your data used?

    Part of the data is collected to ensure the proper functioning of this website. Other data can be used to remember your preferences, to analyze user behavior statistically and to contact you on request. The basis for data processing is here Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR. This article allows the processing of data to fulfill a contract or pre-contractual measures.

    What rights do you have regarding to your data?

    As permitted by law, you always have the right to request information about your stored data, its origin, its recipients, and the purpose of its collection at no charge. You also have the right to request that the data will be corrected, blocked or deleted. Furthermore, you have the right to file a complaint with the responsible supervisory authority.

    If you have more questions about the issue of privacy and data protection on this website, contact the site operator, using the address given in the legal notice, at any time.

    General and mandatory information

    Responsible party

    The responsible party is the natural or legal person who alone or together with others decides on the purposes and means of processing personal data (e. g. names, email addresses, etc.).

    On this website, the site operator and third parties carry out the data processing. The operator's contact data can be found in the website's required legal notice.

    Revocation of your consent to data processing

    Many data processing operations are only possible with your express consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time. An informal notification by email to the website operator is sufficient. The legality of the data processing carried out before the revocation remains unaffected by the revocation.

    Right to object the data collection in special cases and direct advertising (Art. 21 GDPR)

    If the data processing is based on Art. 6 (1) (e) or (f) GDPR, you have always the right to object the processing of your personal data for reasons that arise from your particular situation; this also applies to profiling based on these provisions. The respective legal basis can be found in this data protection policy.

    If you lodge an objection, the website operator will no longer process your personal data concerned, unless he can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for the processing that outweigh your interests, rights and freedoms or the processing serves to assert, exercise or defend legal claims (contradiction according to Art. 21 (1) GDPR).

    If your personal data are processed for direct advertising purposes, you have always the right to object this; this also applies to profiling insofar as it is connected to a direct advertising. If you object, then your personal data will no longer be used for direct advertising purposes (contradiction according to Art. 21 (2) GDPR).

    Right to file complaints with supervisory authority

    In the event of violations of the GDPR, those affected have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the country of their habitual residence, their place of work or the place of the alleged violation.

    The right to lodge a complaint is without prejudice to other administrative or judicial remedies.

    Right to data portability

    You have the right that the processed data, based on your consent or in fulfillment of a contract, will be delivered to you or a third party in a standard, machine-readable format.

    A direct transfer of your data to another party will only be done if it is technically feasible.

    SSL or TLS encryption

    This website uses SSL or TLS encryption for security reasons and for the protection of the transmission of confidential content, such as your inquiries send to the site operator. You can recognize an encrypted connection by the fact that the address line of the browser changes from “http: //” to “https: //” and by the displayed lock icon.

    If SSL or TLS encryption is activated, third parties cannot read the data that you transmit to the website operator.

    Information, blocking and deletion

    As permitted by law, you have always the right to request information about your stored data, its origin, its recipients, and the purpose of its collection at no charge according to Art. 15 GDPR. You also have the right to request that this data will be corrected according to Art. 16 GDPR and blocked or deleted according to Art. 17 GDPR.

    If you have further questions about this topic, contact the website operator at any time using the address given in the legal notice.

    Right to restrict the data processing

    You have always the right to request that the processing of your personal data will be restricted in accordance with Art. 18 GDPR. For that purpose, contact the website operator using the address given in the legal notice.

    The right to restrict processing exists in the following cases:

    • If you dispute the correctness of your personal data stored by the website operator, you have the right to request a restriction for the checking period, because it usually takes some time to check this.
    • If the processing of your personal data happened/happens unlawfully, you have the right to request a restriction.
    • If the website operator no longer needs your personal data, but you need them to exercise, defend or assert legal claims, you have the right to request a restriction and to demand that your data will not be deleted.
    • If you have lodged an objection in accordance with Art. 21 (1) GDPR, a balance has to be made between your interests and the interests of the website operator. As long as it is not clear whose interests outweigh, you have the right to request a restriction.

    If you have restricted the processing of your personal data, this data - apart from its storage - may only be processed with your consent or to assert, exercise or defend legal claims or to protect the rights of another natural or legal person or for reasons of important public interest of the European Union or a member state.

    Objection to the contact data usage

    According to Section 28 BDSG, the website operator objects to any commercial usage and disclosure of his personal data, given in the legal notice. This includes every direct marketing, advertising broadcasting or market and opinion researching.

    He expressly reserves the right to take legal actions in any event of an unsolicited sending of advertising and information material, such as spam emails or mailings.

    Changes and updates to this privacy policy

    The website operator requests you to inform yourself regularly about the content of this privacy policy. Because, he adapts this policy as soon as changes in his data processing make it necessary. If the changes require e. g. an approval from you or another individual notification, he will inform you.

    Data collection on this website

    By visiting

    The website operator stores the following data in your browser's storages.

    Cookies:

    • "consent": This cookie is used to check if you have already seen the consent management window (max-age: 1 month).

    Local Storage:

    • "yPos": This variable is necessary to remember your current position before a reloading of the website.
    • "embed-youtube-videos": This variable is used to check if you have agreed to embed YouTube videos.

    You have the opportunity to set the preferences of your browser so that you will be informed about the storing and only allow it in individual cases or exclude it in general. You can also activate there an automatic clearing. If you refuse the storing, the functionality of this website is limited.

    By contacting

    If you contact the website operator, e. g. via email, your request including all personal data resulting from it (name, request, etc.) will be saved and processed by him for the purpose of processing your request. This data will not be passed on without your consent.

    The data will be processed based on Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR, if your request is related to a contract fulfillment or is necessary to carry out pre-contractual measures. In all other cases, the processing will be based on your consent (Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR) and/or on the legitimate interest of the website operator (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR), because he has a legitimate interest in the effective processing of inquiries addressed to him.

    Your data remain by the website operator until you demand a deletion, revoke your consent or the purpose no longer applies (e. g. after your request has been processed).

    Data processing by social networks

    The website operator maintains publicly accessible profiles on social media networks. The networks used are listed here.

    Social media networks analyze user behavior comprehensively when their website is visited. Consequently, if you visit the website operator's social media presences, numerous data processing processes can be triggered. E. g., if you are logged into a social media account at the same time, the network providers can assign your visit to your account too. However, your personal data can also be recorded if you are not logged in or if you haven't an account. In these cases, the data will be collected e. g. through cookies that are stored on your end devices or by recording your IP address.

    Based on the collected data, the network providers can create user profiles in which your preferences and interests are stored. They can use this profiles to show you interest-based advertising inside and outside of the networks and on all end devices, on which you are/were logged in.

    It should also be noticed that the website operator can't comprehend all data processing processes of the network providers. Depending on the provider, further data processing processes may be carried out. Details about this can be found in their specific terms of use and privacy policy.

    The website operator's social media presences are intended to ensure a wide presence of his online offer. This is a legitimate interest according to Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR.

    The analyze processes initiated by the network providers may be based on a different legal bases. They have to specify their legal bases by themselves (e. g. the consent according to Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR).

    Responsibilities and assertion of rights

    The website operator and the network providers are both responsible for the data processing processes triggered through a visit to a social media presence. In principle, users can exercise their rights in terms of information, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability and complaint against the website operator as well as against the network providers.

    It should be noted that despite the joint responsibility, the website operator does not have full influence on the data processing processes of the network providers. His possibilities depend largely on the corporate policy of the providers.

    Storage periods

    The data recorded directly by the website operator via the social media presences will be deleted as soon as the purpose of the storage ceases to exist; users request the deletion or revoke their consent to the storage.

    Saved cookies remain on your end devices until you delete them. Mandatory legal provisions - in particular statutory retention periods - remain unaffected.

    The website operator has no influence on the storage periods of personal data that is saved by the network providers for their own purposes. For more information about the collection and use of your data by the network providers as well as your rights and protection options look at their specific privacy policy.
